## Client: Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Platform
## Date: April 18, 2026
## Prepared by: Utkarsha Chandgadkar

---

## Background
Olist is a Brazilian e-commerce platform connecting 
small businesses to major marketplaces. They need a 
data analytics pipeline to understand business 
performance across revenue, delivery, customers 
and sellers.

---

## Business Questions to Answer

### 1. Revenue Analysis
- What is the monthly revenue trend?
- Which product categories generate most revenue?
- Which states have highest order volumes?
- What is the average order value by payment type?

### 2. Delivery Performance
- What is the average delivery time?
- Which sellers have highest late delivery rate?
- Which states have worst delivery performance?
- How does delivery time affect review scores?

### 3. Customer Behavior
- Who are the top spending customers?
- What % of customers are repeat buyers?
- How do customers pay? (credit card vs boleto)
- What is customer distribution by state?

### 4. Seller Performance
- Who are the top 10 sellers by revenue?
- Which sellers have best/worst review scores?
- Which seller states perform best?
