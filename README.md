Built an AI Powered E-Commerce Sales Analytics Pipeline

The project builds an end to end analytics pipeline on the Olist Brazilian E-commerce dataset which is around 550K+ records (8 tables)by using Python, Snowflake and Calude AI.

Raw e-commerce data is ingested, cleaned, and modeled through a Medallion Architecture in Snowflake. Business metrics from the Gold layer are passed to Claude API which generates executive-level insights automatically.

## Architecture 
Bronze → Silver → Gold → Claude AI → Insights
## Tech Stack 
Python, Pandas, Snowflake, Claude API (Anthropic), Tableau, SQL

## 5 KPI's, Business Questions Answered 
1. Which categories have declining revenue over time?
2. What is the customer distribution by state?
3. Which sellers have high revenue but poor delivery?
4. What percentage of revenue comes from top 10 categories?
5. Which seller states have the worst delivery performance?

## AI Layer Gold layer KPI results are passed to Claude API using Python. Claude interprets the business metrics and generates executive insights automatically.

## Key Insights Generated by Claude AI + Business Actions

**Insight 1 - Seasonal Revenue Volatility**
Top categories drop R$28K-R$45K during Q4-Q1 transitions.
Business can launch post-holiday retention campaigns and subscription models to smooth seasonal revenue dips.

**Insight 2 - São Paulo Customer Value Gap**
SP has 41% of customers but lowest avg spend (R$142) vs Bahia (R$181) and Rio (R$166).
Business can run premium upselling campaigns in SP while expanding acquisition in high-value states like BA and SC.

**Insight 3 - High Revenue Poor Delivery Risk**
Top sellers generating R$120K-R$247K delivering in 13-22 days — above platform benchmarks.
Business can enforce delivery SLAs for high-revenue sellers and offer fulfillment center partnerships to reduce delivery times.

**Insight 4 - Revenue Concentration Risk**
10 categories drive 62% of total revenue — high dependency risk.
Business can diversify by creating seller incentive programs for underrepresented categories to reduce concentration exposure.

**Insight 5 - Northern Region Logistics Crisis**
AM, CE, MA sellers show 17-47 day delivery — 3-4x slower than south.
Business can invest in regional fulfillment centers and partner with local logistics providers in the north to close the delivery gap.
